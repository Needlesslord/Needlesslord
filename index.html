Giravolt: VR game in Palau Moja

This is a GitHub repository dedicated to the development of a virtual reality (VR) game that celebrates Catalan heritage and culture. The project aims to create an immersive experience where players can explore the Palau Moja, a historic building in Barcelona, through the lens of technology.
By leveraging 3D digitalization techniques, Giravolt brings the architectural marvels of Catalonia to life in a virtual environment. Players can interact with the virtual Palau Moja, learn about its history, and appreciate its significance—all while promoting awareness of Catalan cultural heritage.
For more information, you can visit the Giravolt project website. 🏰🎮

Dune: Special Spice Ops by Chamfer Studios, video game created for the subject "Project III"

Dune: Special Spice Ops is an exhilarating real-time tactics (RTT) game developed during the spring of 2023 by a talented team of 25 students from the Bachelor’s degree in Video Game Design and Development at the CITM-UPC center in Barcelona. As a game designer, I played a pivotal role in shaping the game’s mechanics, stealth elements, and strategic gameplay.
Inspired by the classic game Commandos (1998) by Pyro Studios, and drawing from the rich science fiction universe of Dune by Frank Herbert, this game invites players to explore the treacherous sands of Arrakis. Stealth, discovery, and strategy are at the heart of the experience as players follow the journey of our protagonist, Zhib, alongside companions Nerala and Omozra. Together, they unravel the mysteries and threats lurking among the people of Arrakis.
For more in-depth details, visit the Dune: Special Spice Ops website. Immerse yourself in the world of colossal sandworms, spice melange, and interstellar intrigue—it’s a journey you won’t want to miss! 🌟🎮🚀

StrawberryEngine: 3D Engine by Nuto Studios for the subject "3D Engines"

This repository is dedicated to developing a 3D Engine for the subject of 3D Engines taught at CITM-UPC. The project is a collaborative effort by Tomás Carreras and Núria Lamonja, under the guidance of Marc Garrigó. The engine serves as a base for further development and includes essential features, like model import with drag-and-drop functionality, texture import with drag-and-drop support, a game object hierarchy, a component pattern, Unity-like camera controls, and improved editor tools. It operates under the MIT License. For more details, you can explore the GitHub repository! 🍓🎮
